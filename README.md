Description: A case study demonstrating the use of AI to generate highly constrained, physically accurate cinematic visual assets while actively managing and overriding model hallucinations.

# AI Asset Generation: Predictive Prompt Chaining

**Objective:** To generate a high-fidelity, physically accurate cinematic teaser poster using Generative AI, demonstrating the ability to manage AI hallucinations and apply targeted spatial constraints.

---

## Phase 1: Ideation & Concepting
Generated 5 distinct thematic variations for a "World War 5 on the Moon" concept. Selected the "Gritty Realism" route to challenge the AI with strict physical constraints. Example-zero-atmosphere physics

## Phase 2: Establishing Core Assets 
Applied a "Secret Structure" prompt using Role, Context, Task, and Constraints. 

**Key Technical Constraints Applied:**
* **Scientific Accuracy:** Explicitly commanded "no explosions in space."in line with chemical explosions being impossible without the presence of oxygen or other common oxidizers
* **Visual Priority:** Positioned blood drops to frame, rather than obscure, the reflection.
* **Psychological Anchor:** Dictated a "shell-shocked" expression to override generic soldier generations.

## Phase 3: The Iteration (Spatial Anchoring & In-Painting)
**The Challenge:** Introduce environmental storytelling without triggering a full AI redraw of the core assets.

**The Solution:** Executed a targeted spatial constraint prompt, forcing the AI to manipulate *only* the lower left and right quadrants of the visor reflection, successfully integrating 5+ fallen soldiers and destroyed sci-fi light machines.

### **Executive Summary**
By utilizing predictive prompt chaining and targeted spatial constraints, I generated a highly specific, studio-grade visual asset while actively managing model hallucinations. This workflow reduced the creative iteration cycle and proved the viability of using programmatic text commands to execute precise visual changes.
